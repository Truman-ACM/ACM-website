ACM (Association of Computing Machinery)

ACM is a computer science organization on campus that focuses on professional development and exploration of different topics through speakers, presentations, and focused interest groups.

We currently have two different interest groups going on focusing on Arduinos (open source hardware) and there is another group working with open source Voice API's.
